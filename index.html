About

Baalveer fights with the evil forces who seek to destroy the planet. He got his powers from parilok and in Baalveer Returns, he has a new partner. His name is Vivan.

It started premiering on 10 September 2019 on Sony SAB, starring Dev Joshi, and Vansh Sayani in the titular lead roles of Baalveers. It is a sequel of Baal Veer, which ran from 2012 to 2016. It is produced by Optimystix Entertainment with screenplay by Manoj Tripathi.
